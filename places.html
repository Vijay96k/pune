Tourist Places in Pune

Pune is a beautiful city known for its rich history, culture, and natural beauty. It offers a mix of historical monuments, forts, and scenic spots.

Top Attractions

  1. Shaniwar Wada
  2. Sinhagad Fort
  3. Aga Khan Palace
  4. Pashan Lake
  5. Lavasa City

Detailed Information

  • Shaniwar Wada: Built in 1732, it was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire. It is famous for its grand architecture and historical importance. The fort is known for its grand gates and strong walls. It played an important role in Maratha history. Today, it is a popular tourist attraction.
  • Sinhagad Fort: Located on a hill, this fort is known for the battle of Tanaji Malusare. It offers a great trekking experience and beautiful views of the surroundings. Sinhagad Fort is a famous hill fort located near Pune. It is known for the historic battle of Tanaji Malusare. The fort is situated on a hill and offers beautiful scenic views. It is a popular spot for trekking and picnics. Today, it attracts many tourists and history lovers.
  • Aga Khan Palace: Built in 1892, it is an important historical site where Mahatma Gandhi was imprisoned during the freedom struggle. Aga Khan Palace is a famous historical monument in Pune. It was built in 1892 by Sultan Aga Khan. The palace is known for its beautiful architecture and large gardens. Mahatma Gandhi and other freedom fighters were kept here during the freedom struggle. Today, it is an important tourist attraction and museum.
  • Pashan Lake: A peaceful and scenic lake, ideal for bird watching and relaxing in nature. Pashan Lake is a beautiful lake located in Pune. It was built during the British era to supply water to the city. The lake is known for its peaceful environment and natural beauty. It is a popular spot for bird watching and morning walks. Many nature lovers visit the lake to relax and enjoy the scenery.
  • Lavasa City: A planned hill city near Pune, known for its colorful buildings and beautiful lakeside views. It is a popular tourist destination.Lavasa is a planned hill city located near Pune in Maharashtra. It is known for its beautiful scenery, colorful buildings, and lakeside views. The city is inspired by an Italian town and offers activities like boating, cycling, and sightseeing. It is a popular weekend destination for tourists and nature lovers.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Pune is from October to February when the weather is pleasant.
